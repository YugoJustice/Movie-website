Underwater

2020, Action / Adventure / Drama / Horror / Sci-Fi / Thriller

Synopsis:


The Kepler 822, a research and drilling facility operating in the depths of the Mariana Trench, is pushing its way into the centre of the Earth. When a mysterious earthquake strikes the site and destroys most of the deep-water station, mechanical engineer Norah and a handful of survivors must put on their heavy diving suits to walk across the dangerous, pitch-black ocean floor to find a way out. Now left with meagre choices, the deep-sea divers need to keep a clear head to deal with high pressure and the perils of meandering through the unrecognisable facility's scattered debris. However, the vast, unexplored ocean harbours deadly secrets--something strange is lurking in the abyss, awakened by the intruders.
