Deadpool & Wolverine

2024, Action / Adventure / Comedy / Sci-Fi

Synopsis:


Wade Wilson leaves behind the mantle of Deadpool, adopts a normal civilian life and also gets a job at a pre-owned car agency, but when he is approached by a man named Paradox, from the Time Variance Authority, he offers Wade to join the Marvel Cinematic Universe. But as Wade finds out that Paradox wants to vaporize his Timeline, he suits up again and embarks on a journey of comedy and laughter when he travels the Multiverse to find an alternate version of Wolverine, who Deadpool recruits to convince him to help Deadpool save his Timeline from the existential threat that it's facing. It is then when Deadpool learns the true nature of heroism; and becomes best friends with Wolverine.
