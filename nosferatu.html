Nosferatu

2024, Fantasy / Horror / Mystery

Synopsis:


A gothic tale of obsession between a haunted young woman and the terrifying vampire infatuated with her, causing untold horror in its wake.
A young Ellen, longing for love, prays to any spirit to grant her companionship. Her plea is answered by a terrifying beastly vision that causes her to collapse in a violent seizure. Years later, in 1838, Ellen lives in Wisborg, Germany, with her husband, Thomas Hutter, an estate agent. Thomas, eager to secure a lucrative deal, accepts an assignment to sell a crumbling castle in Wisborg to the enigmatic Count Orlok, unaware that this arrangement is part of a sinister pact between Orlok and his employer, Herr Knock. Despite Ellen's ominous warnings, including a confession of a pre-wedding dream in which her bridegroom was Death, Thomas departs for Orlok's castle in the Carpathian Mountains, leaving Ellen in the care of his friend Friedrich Harding.
