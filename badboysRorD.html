Bad Boys: Ride or Die

2024, Action / Adventure / Comedy / Crime / Thriller

Synopsis:


Four years after the death of Isabel Aretas, MPD Detective Mike Lowrey marries his physical therapist, Christine. During the wedding reception, his partner, Detective Marcus Burnett, suffers a mild heart attack and falls into a coma. While unconscious, Marcus has a vision of their late Captain Conrad Howard, who reassures him that his time has not yet come.
When Marcus wakes up, he becomes convinced he cannot die. Shortly afterward, a news report accuses Captain Howard of being tied to drug cartel corruption, tarnishing his legacy. Determined to clear Howard's name, Mike and Marcus enlist the help of Captain Rita Secada and her boyfriend, mayoral candidate Adam Lockwood. The investigation leads them to Mike's estranged son, Armando Aretas, who is incarcerated for Howard's murder. Armando reveals that Howard was not corrupt but was close to exposing those who were. To protect themselves, the real culprits hired Armando and Isabel to kill him. Armando offers to help identify the conspirators.
