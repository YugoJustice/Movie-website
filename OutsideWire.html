Outside the Wire

2021, Action / Adventure / Fantasy / Sci-Fi

Synopsis:


In 2036, there is a war between pro-Russian insurgents and the resistance in Ukraine. US troops are the peacekeeping forces. The drone pilot Lieutenant Thomas Harp disobeys direct orders from his superior and kills two Marines to save thirty-eight soldiers bombing the enemy. He goes to court martial and is sent to Camp Nathaniel in Ukraine as punishment to work directly with the notorious Captain Leo. Soon Harp learns that Capt. Leo is an android and is assigned to go with him to deliver cholera vaccines to the resistance and learn the whereabouts of the terrorist Victor Koval from an informer. Viktor has the intention to activate the nuclear silos and launch missiles in USA. Harp helps Leo to hunt down Viktor, but he learns that Leo has a personal agenda to accomplish.
