Justice League: Warworld

2023, Action / Adventure / Animation / Fantasy / Sci-Fi

Synopsis:


Until now, the Justice League has been a loose association of superpowered individuals. But when they are swept away to Warworld, a place of unending brutal gladiatorial combat, Batman, Superman, Wonder Woman and the others must somehow unite to form an unbeatable resistance able to lead an entire planet to freedom.
The Justice League is kidnapped and transported to Warworld, a planet of endless gladiatorial combat. To survive, Batman, Superman, and Wonder Woman must unite to form a resistance. They rally the enslaved population against the oppressive regime. Facing insurmountable odds, the heroes lead a rebellion against the tyrannical ruler of Warworld. Through courage and ingenuity, the Justice League inspires hope and ultimately liberates the planet from its brutal fate.
