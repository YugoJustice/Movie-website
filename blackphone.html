The Black Phone

2021, Horror / Mystery / Thriller

Synopsis:


A vicious killer known as the grabber kidnaps a 13 year old boy and locks him up in a soundproof basement. He starts receiving phone calls from the killers past victims. Eventually, he starts finding a way out all on his own and kills the grabber.
North Denver, 1978. As the dreadful infamy of the stealthy child abductor known as The Grabber spreads across the city, terrorising its residents, tight-knit siblings Finney and Gwen live in fear of two things: the masked serial killer and their abusive father. When the monstrous Grabber kidnaps yet another defenceless boy, two local detectives embark on a time-sensitive mission to shed light on the knotty child disappearance cases, trying to piece insubstantial evidence together. However, the disconnected black rotary telephone on the wall of the deranged kidnapper's well-soundproofed basement keeps ringing. But who's on the other end of the line?
