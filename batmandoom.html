Batman: The Doom That Came to Gotham

2023, Action / Animation / Fantasy / Horror

Synopsis:


Explorer Bruce Wayne accidentally unleashes an ancient evil, and returns to Gotham after being away for two decades. There, Batman battles Lovecraftian supernatural forces and encounters allies and enemies such as Green Arrow, Ra's al Ghul, Mr. Freeze, Killer Croc, Two-Face and James Gordon.
After a 20-year absence, Bruce Wayne returns to Gotham City. He discovers a doomsday cult planning to unleash ancient magic and demonic forces upon the city. Forced to become Batman once more, he battles these supernatural threats while struggling to resist the corrupting influence of the Old Gods. The film explores themes of sanity, fear, and the darkness that lurks within Gotham itself.
